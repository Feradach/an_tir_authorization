Forms

These are the authorization forms. You can also access these, and the rule books, by going to the An Tir Library.

If you use the paper forms, they can be scanned and emailed to the Martial Authorization Officer.

When sending in an authorization if the person being authorized is a minor or isn't a member, they need to sign the appropriate waiver as well.
