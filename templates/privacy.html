Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-05

The An Tir Martial Authorization Database is used to manage martial authorization records, officer workflows, waivers, supporting documents, and related account access for the Kingdom of An Tir.

Information We Collect

The system may store information needed to identify participants and manage authorization status, including:

  • legal and society names;
  • contact information, including email addresses;
  • branch, region, membership, waiver, and authorization details;
  • marshal or officer roles;
  • supporting documents uploaded for authorization workflows;
  • account, login, and security event records needed to operate the site.

How Information Is Used

Information in this system is used to:

  • confirm martial authorization status;
  • support marshal, regional, and kingdom officer review workflows;
  • allow users and officers to maintain accurate records;
  • send account, recovery, and authorization-related emails;
  • protect the database from misuse, spam, unauthorized access, and operational failure.

Who Can Access Information

Public visitors may be able to view authorization information intended for field and event use. Account details, officer tools, supporting documents, and administrative functions are limited by login status and role-based permissions.

Information Sharing

We do not sell personal information or share it with third parties except as required by law, as needed to operate and secure the system, or as needed for legitimate Kingdom authorization, marshal, officer, and event operations.

Supporting Documents

Supporting documents are used only for authorization-related workflows. Access is limited to the account holder and authorized officers or administrators who need the information to perform their duties.

Email

The system sends operational emails such as account setup instructions, password reset links, and authorization workflow notices. During the production launch transition, email may come from a temporary Gmail account announced through official Kingdom communication channels.

Data Protection

The system uses role-based access controls, secure transport, operational logging, rate limiting, and backup procedures to protect the database. No online system can guarantee perfect security.

Data Corrections

If your record is incorrect, contact the appropriate authorization officer through the contact information published on this site or through official Kingdom channels.

Changes To This Policy

This policy may be updated as the system moves from emergency launch operations to long-term Kingdom-managed infrastructure and email.
