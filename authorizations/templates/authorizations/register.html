Account creation rules

Before you begin
  • Email, username, first name, last name, address, city, state/province, postal code, and branch are required.
  • Username must be unique (no duplicates), use 150 characters or fewer, and may contain letters, digits, and the symbols @, ., +, -, and _. You can use the same email as another user.
  • Phone number must be 10 digits; formatting is applied automatically.
  • Postal code must be within An Tir: Canadian codes starting with V, or U.S. ZIPs starting with 97, 98, or in the ranges 991–994, 838, or 835.
  • Membership number and expiration must be provided together, or leave both blank. The experiation date must be in the future.
  • Membership number must be between 1 and 20 digits.
  • If registering a minor, a birthday is required. Non‑minors must not list a parent.
  • Title is optional; you may choose from the list or enter a new title with a rank.
  • After this is submitted, you will receive a password setup link via email.
  • After you set your password, log in and go to "My Account" to sign your waiver. After that you will be able to add authorizations to yourself.
